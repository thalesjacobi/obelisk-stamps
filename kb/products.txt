Products & Services - Obelisk Stamps

Obelisk Stamps is a UK-based e-commerce site that offers artisanal framed stamp collections. Each piece features authentic postage stamps carefully arranged and presented in handcrafted frames.

Our framed stamp displays are organised by theme. Some themes include:
- Birds and wildlife
- Flowers and botanicals
- Fishing and outdoor pursuits
- Wine and gastronomy
- Royalty and historical figures
- Transport (trains, ships, aviation)
- Space exploration
- Sports
- Art and architecture

As each piece is handmade, slight variations in appearance may occur, making every frame unique.

All frames are presentation-ready and ideal as gifts. If you would like a gift note included, please add your message at checkout.

Authenticity:
Each stamp used in our framed displays is an authentic postage stamp sourced from reputable dealers and collections. Where possible, historical context and issue details are included.
