Returns & Refunds - Obelisk Stamps

We want you to love your purchase. If for any reason you are not completely satisfied, you may return your item within 14 days of delivery.

To be eligible for a return:
- The item must be unused and in its original condition.
- It must be returned in its original packaging.

To initiate a return, please contact us via our contact form at obelisk-stamps.com/contact. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is faulty or incorrect.

Damaged or Faulty Items:
If your item arrives damaged:
1. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery.
2. Provide clear photos of the packaging and item.
3. We will arrange a replacement or full refund.

We take packaging very seriously, but if something goes wrong, we will resolve it quickly. Refunds are processed within 5-10 working days of receiving the returned item and will be issued to the original payment method.
