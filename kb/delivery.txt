Delivery & Shipping - Obelisk Stamps

All Obelisk Stamps frames are carefully handcrafted and prepared to order.

Processing Time:
- Orders are prepared within 2-4 working days.
- During peak seasons, this may extend slightly. We will always notify you.

UK Shipping:
- Standard Tracked Delivery (2-3 working days after dispatch) costs £4.95.
- Free UK delivery on orders over £75.
- All parcels are fully tracked and securely packaged.

International Shipping:
- Europe: 5-10 working days.
- Rest of World: 7-14 working days.
- Shipping costs are calculated at checkout.
- Customs duties (if applicable) are the buyer's responsibility.

Each frame is wrapped in protective materials and boxed securely to ensure it arrives in pristine condition. Tracking information is provided via email once your order has been dispatched.
